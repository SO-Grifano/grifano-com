Your Journey to Success:
A Step-by-Step Web Design Process

1

Friendly Chat

We kick things off with a relaxed chat where I learn all about your business, the challenges you're facing, and the dreams you're chasing. It's my job as web architect to figure out exactly what you need from your website.

2

Smart Planning

Next, I roll up my sleeves and get to planning. Together, we’ll come up with a game plan that keeps your audience and competition in mind, setting the stage for a website that truly resonates.

3

Sketching Ideas

With a plan in place, I start sketching the basic layout of your site. It's like drawing the blueprint of a house, ensuring everything is in the right place and looks just as you envisioned.

4

Bringing Your Site to Life

Here, the real fun begins! I use tools like Affinity Designer and Figma to craft detailed, yet inviting designs. Then, I use Webflow to build a website that's not only beautiful but fast and secure too.

5

Handing Over the Keys

As we wrap up, I hand the reins over to you with some handy video tutorials. This way, you can manage your site effortlessly, with tips on everything from updating content to improving SEO.

6

Ongoing Care & Support

Need quick content updates or new features added? I'm here to help. Feel free to choose a plan that suits your needs or contact me for a customised approach.

Common questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions.

How do I get started?

Simply contact me through the form or book a meeting. We'll discuss your needs and I'll offer a customized quote and timeline.

What is the cost?

Cost varies based on design complexity, page count, and special features. After discussing your needs, I'll provide a clear quote.

How long does it take?

Time varies by project complexity. Simple sites can take three days; more complex ones, longer. I'll estimate a timeline in our initial chat.

I have a design, can you build it?

Certainly! I specialize in turning designs into functional Webflow sites, or code it within HTMS, CSS and JavaScript. Buy domain name, and deploy your website to hosting. We'll discuss timeline and budget before starting.

Do you offer full SEO services?

Yes, I can provide a full SEO campaign that include keyword research, page optimisation, and more. We should discus it, as a SEO campaign has a long term service.

What about copyrights?

You own all project rights upon completion. Note: stock assets used can't be trademarked. For specific legal needs, consult a lawyer.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out to me at any time.
